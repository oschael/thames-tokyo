## Page-by-Page Content Strategy

### 1. index.html - Homepage
**Purpose**: Establish premium positioning and introduce the access problem
**Key Sections**:
- Hero: "Curating the inaccessible" with typewriter animation
- Trust Challenge: Interactive three-column friction points
- Founder Authority: Portrait with credibility bullets
- Services Preview: Three solution cards
- Delicatessen Teaser: 3 flagship products (subtle)
- Access Stories Preview: One anonymized case study
- Contact CTA: "Discuss your access challenge"

**Interactive Elements**:
- Typewriter hero tagline
- Trust challenge hover expansion
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Product teaser carousel

### 2. about.html - Founder Story
**Purpose**: Build trust through Rie's story and brand philosophy
**Key Sections**:
- Hero Quote: Brand manifesto "The Art of Access"
- Founder Credibility: Timeline with career milestones
- The Problem: Iceberg diagram of invisible market layers
- The Solution: Cultural interface process visualization
- Execution: Peace of mind guarantees
- Vision: UK-Japan bridge philosophy

**Interactive Elements**:
- Clickable timeline with content reveals
- Modular messaging block animations
- Process diagram with step highlights

### 3. services.html - Service Offerings
**Purpose**: Detail the three-pillar approach to cultural interface
**Key Sections**:
- Introduction: Problem context setting
- How We Work: Three service pillars
  - Curation & Vetting
  - Cultural Interface
  - End-to-End Execution
- Peace of Mind: Execution guarantees
- Sector Expertise: Industry grid (Pharma, Tech, Finance, Marketing, Consumer)
- Access Stories: Link to full case studies
- Delicatessen Connection: Quality proof point

**Interactive Elements**:
- Service pillar hover details
- Sector filter functionality
- Case study preview modals

### 4. access-stories.html - Case Studies
**Purpose**: Demonstrate real outcomes and unlocked doors
**Key Sections**:
- Hero: "Stories from the Invisible Market"
- Case Study Browser: Filter by sector
- Featured Stories: 5-6 anonymized case studies
- Outcome Metrics: Visual data presentation
- Process Insights: Cultural interface learnings
- CTA: "Start your access story"

**Interactive Elements**:
- Sector-based filtering
- Expandable case study details
- Data visualization charts
- Success metrics animation

### 5. delicatessen.html - Product Showcase
**Purpose**: Tangible proof of sourcing quality and relationships
**Key Sections**:
- Hero: "Taste the Access" with regional map
- Regional Explorer: Interactive Japan map
- Product Categories:
  - Kyoto: The Art of Exclusivity (5 items)
  - Niigata: The Purity of Prefecture (4 items)
  - Artisanal Condiments: The Flavor Keys (4 items)
  - Regional Heritage: The Craft Stories (2 items)
- Access Stories: Why each product is inaccessible
- Artisan Profiles: Producer relationships
- Business CTA: "Discuss sourcing for your business"

**Interactive Elements**:
- Interactive Japan map with regions
- Product detail modals with access stories
- Region-based filtering
- Add to inquiry (not cart) functionality

### 6. contact.html - Lead Generation
**Purpose**: Convert qualified prospects into conversations
**Key Sections**:
- Contact Form: Name, Company, Sector, Challenge, Urgency
- Trust Reinforcement: Founder and execution blocks
- Direct Contact: rie@thamesandtokyo.com
- Response Promise: 24-hour personal review
- Confidentiality Assurance: Business sensitivity handling

**Interactive Elements**:
- Progressive form with validation
- Sector selection dropdown
- Urgency slider
- Success confirmation

## Technical Implementation

### Core Libraries Integration
- **Anime.js**: Page transitions, element reveals
- **Splitting.js**: Text animations for quotes
- **Typed.js**: Hero tagline typewriter
- **ECharts.js**: Case study data visualization
- **p5.js**: Background textures, subtle animations
- **Splide.js**: Product carousels, image galleries
- **Pixi.js**: Interactive Japan map

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- Breakpoints: 320px, 768px, 1024px, 1440px
- Touch-optimized interactions
- Performance-optimized images

### Performance Optimization
- Lazy loading for images
- Minified CSS/JS
- Optimized animations
- Fast loading times

## Content Requirements

### Text Content
- Brand manifesto (150 words)
- Founder story (800 words)
- Service descriptions (300 words each)
- Case studies (400 words each)
- Product access stories (150 words each)
- Artisan profiles (200 words each)

### Visual Content
- Hero images: 6 high-quality photographs
- Product photography: 15 individual product shots
- Founder portraits: 3 professional shots
- Cultural imagery: 10 supporting photographs
- Data visualizations: 5 custom charts
- Interactive map: 1 Japan regional map

### Interactive Components
- Trust challenge expander
- Founder timeline
- Case study browser
- Product region explorer
- Contact form with validation
- Newsletter signup

This outline ensures every element serves the core narrative of positioning Thames&Tokyo as the authoritative bridge to Japan's exclusive suppliers, with the delicatessen serving as tangible proof of access rather than a primary revenue driver.
